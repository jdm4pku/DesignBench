The vehicle safety and security component system mainly includes the vehicle’s interior and body assembly parts. The interior is equipped with an alarm system, seat belts, front row seats, and a driver’s airbag, while the body assembly comprises the vehicle body, bumper, and keyless entry system. The safety features are primarily reflected in the configuration of seat belts, airbags, and bumpers, which enhance the protection of the driver and passengers during driving. The security features are realized through the installation of an onboard alarm and a keyless entry system, providing support for anti-theft and convenient access for users. The system integrates safety and security functions, aiming to offer users comprehensive safety protection and a convenient user experience.
